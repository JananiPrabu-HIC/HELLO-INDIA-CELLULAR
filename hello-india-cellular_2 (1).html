Quick Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? Find answers to the most common queries from our customers.

What are your business hours? ▼

We are open Monday to Saturday from 10:00 AM to 8:30 PM, and on Sundays from 10:00 AM to 1:00 PM. We are closed on major public holidays — do call ahead on holidays to confirm.

Do you sell genuine / original mobile phones? ▼

Absolutely. We only stock genuine, brand-authorised products. We have built our reputation over 21 years on trust and honesty — you will always get what you pay for at Hello India Cellular.

Do you support all mobile networks for recharge? ▼

Yes! We provide instant recharge services for all major networks including Jio, Airtel, BSNL, and Vi (Vodafone Idea). Walk in or WhatsApp us for quick service.

Can you help me port my number to a different network? ▼

Yes, we assist with Mobile Number Portability (MNP) for all operators. Just bring your existing SIM and a valid ID proof. We'll guide you through the entire process and activate your new connection.

Do you offer mobile repair services? ▼

We offer basic mobile repair services including screen replacement, battery replacement, and charging port repair for common handsets. Visit the shop with your device for a free inspection and quote.

What payment methods do you accept? ▼

We accept cash, UPI (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm), and major debit/credit cards. For large purchases, we can also arrange easy EMI options through select banks.

How long has Hello India Cellular been in business? ▼

Hello India Cellular was established on 26 December 2004 by Mr. D. Prabu. As of 2025, we have been serving our valued customers for over 21 years — making us one of the most experienced local mobile shops in the area.
